Key Formulas

Essay Hypothesis Summary

How changing G or ℏ shifts the gravity-quantum balance.

Core Formulas

  • P_gravity ∝ G M²/R⁴
  • P_Pauli ∝ ℏ² ρ^(5/3) / m

Stability Condition

P_Pauli ≥ P_gravity → ℏ² ∝ G → ℏ ∝ √G

Key Statement from Essay

To maintain balance at G × 10³⁶, ℏ must increase by 10¹⁸.

Key Scaling Relations

  • G × 10³⁶ → ℏ must × 10¹⁸
  • Without ℏ compensation: early collapse
  • G/ℏ² sets atomic gravity strength

Implications

  • In our universe: G/ℏ² is tiny → gravity negligible at atomic scales
  • With G × 10³⁶ (no ℏ change): matter collapses at much larger scales
  • Balance ℏ ∝ √G: stellar structure and chemistry remain possible
